Introduction

DECENTRABOARD or the 'ENVOY Billboard' is the first NFT curated billboard that has the ambition to be on display year round in the most iconic and unique locations around the globe.

Furthermore, DECENTRABOARD is the centerpiece of the extraordinary 'Cryptopolis' artwork. By claiming your spot on the billboard you'll be able to showcase your brand, company or gimmick in both the digital and real world.

That's not all, by showcasing DECENTRABOARD on the most iconic locations in the world it obviously will become one of the most well known NFT fueled billboards in the world. Each slot is minted as an NFT and comes with an unique rare 1/1040 art piece of your slot, that grants you the rights on publishing images on the billboard.
